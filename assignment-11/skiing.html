SKIING IN THE ALPS

Jumping

Do not miss out on Jumps. Sometimes it does look scary but it is actually super fun. Just pay attention to a few details so your ski day doesn‘t end in the hospital. 1. Make sure to lean forward when you jump. Don‘t get caught up in a suspine position, you‘ll most likely not be able to land the jump. 2. Try to find the perfect speed when you approach a jump. If you go to fast, it‘ll be harder to land. If you go to slow, you will land on a flat surface. This will hurt both your knees and back.
